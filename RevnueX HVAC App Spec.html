03 Foundation · Product summary

Every screen serves one closed loop.

The marketing site sells a promise: money stops leaking through unanswered phones. The app must make that promise visible and continuous. Every screen should answer "what's the next dollar?" within 2–3 seconds.

01
Lead
Web form pin or missed call
02
AI call
Iris dials in < 60s
03
Qualify
Urgency + window
04
Dispatch
Nearest tech routed
05
Job
Estimate + checklist
06
Paid
Tap to pay · revenue ticks

Design pillars

Speed. Every primary action is one tap from the home screen. No nested menus.

Voice

Money is alive. Counters tick. Pulses on live items. Revenue is the headline of every screen it can be.

Restraint

Dark-first, ember-as-signal. Color earns its place. If it's ember, it's actionable or urgent.

Audience

Not enterprise. Contractor in a truck cab on a 5" screen at noon, sweat on the glass.
